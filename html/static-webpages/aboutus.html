OUR COMPANY

Kukus Corner is an online Gift shop. Especially, designed to allow patrons to purchase gifts for special occasions and to have them shipped worldwide to friends and family. Customers are invited to shop our online store for Delicious Cakes, Fresh Flowers, Chocolates and Toys. Come often for a visit, and let Kukus Corner make your near and dear ones feel special.

Founded in 2006, Kukuscorner is headquartered in Silicon Valley, California, USA with additional offshore facilities in Mumbai, India. Solutions from Kukuscorner are helping organizations to reduce cost and scale up operations, while focusing on their core competency. Our mission is to be a leading IT Products and Services company, that uses human ingenuity and passion for technology to design and implement innovative, cost effective and socially responsible business solutions. We commit ourselves to high standards of integrity in contributing to the best interests of our clients, associates, employees and our communities. We strive for significant returns to our stockholders, through persistence in innovation and operational excellence.
